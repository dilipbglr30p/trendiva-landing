Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 2, 2025

At Trendiva Styles, we respect your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your personal information that we have collected through our services.

How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like us to delete any personal information we have collected about you, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us: Send a request to our support team by email at support@trendivastyles.org. Please include your full name, email address, and any other relevant details to help us locate your data.
  2. Verify Your Identity: For security reasons, we may need to verify your identity before processing your request to prevent unauthorized deletion.
  3. Specify Your Request: Clearly state which data you want deleted or if you want all your personal data removed.

What Happens After You Submit a Deletion Request?

Once we receive your request and verify your identity, we will:

  • Delete the requested personal information from our systems within a reasonable time frame.
  • Confirm with you via email once the deletion is complete.
  • Inform you if we are unable to delete certain information due to legal or operational reasons.

Important Considerations

  • We may retain some information if required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as fraud prevention or to comply with legal obligations.
  • Deleting your data may affect your ability to use certain features or services on our website.
